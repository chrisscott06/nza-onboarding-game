# NET ZERO HERO — Story Bible (v1.1)

*The complete narrative arc. Four worlds, one level each = a finished Climate
Action Plan. Build World 1 now; the rest is locked vision Will & Imi build
against.*

> Filed in the repo as the narrative source of truth. **Name note:** the
> recurring foil is **"Mr Net Stupid Zero"** (spaced, not "bloody"; the source
> draft hyphenated it — kept spaced to match Chris's instruction and the
> in-game label).

Voice: planet's in trouble, we're cheeky about it. British, dry, warm. Funny
about the problem, never bleak. The four-pillar Climate Action Plan is the frame,
worn lightly. The hero's an underdog. **The deep message (the decodED thesis): a
Climate Action Plan is too big for one person — you win by getting everyone
involved and getting real data into the right hands.**

## 1. The hero
You play a **Student** who just starts. Your face goes on the character (face
system built). Character-choice foundation: path two is the **Sustainability
Lead** (overwhelm/isolation vs the Student's powerlessness) — stubbed for later.
Arc: start alone + underpowered; each world you gain an ally + a chunk of real
data; you win by rallying everyone, not soloing it.

## 2. The recurring cast
Data motif: weak when guessing, strong with real data; power-ups are knowledge.
Per world: one **goodie** joins, one **foil** heckles, one **boss** at the end.
- **Foil — Mr Net Stupid Zero** — inertia + petty villainy ("too hard, too
  expensive"; digs for oil, takes bribes, slaps on levies). Triggers the dark
  atmosphere shift. Caricature of a *stance*, never a real person.
- **Big bad — The Oil Baron** — top-hatted fossil tycoon; W1 boss and the hand
  behind every world, beaten for good in W4.

## 4. WORLD 1 — "Power Up the Grid" (BUILD THIS)

### 4.1 Theme (broader than the grid)
Energy (solar/wind/storage — built); Transport (ICE-car hazards + an **EV ride**
power-up); Supply chain/aviation (background plane/trucks); Waste (recycling-bin
collectible). Signature mechanic stays the **storage meter**.

### 4.2 Level shape (Celeste/Mario teaching curve)
Open dim & dirty → meet ally (brightens) → storage section → transport/EV stretch
→ the heckler (turns the level dark) → supply-chain gauntlet (grid-surge dash) →
Oil Baron boss → win (snaps clean-blue; Ed Megawatt salutes; Baron scarpers).

### 4.3 The staged narrative (cutscenes, not text bars)
**Key principle:** narrative is a **staged scene**, not a scrolling text bar.
Each beat: action pauses, the character **walks into frame**, a portrait exchange
plays (name + cheeky lines), the character **exits in a funny way**, play resumes.
Zelda/Paper-Mario theatre. **And the world reacts** — foil arrives → palette cold
& grimy, light drops, music sours; hero wins the argument → re-brighten.

- **BEAT 1 — START. Ed Megawatt sets the mission.** Energy minister in
  hi-vis-over-a-suit. Gives the first datum (offshore wind 41% capacity factor).
  Funny exit: a turbine blade sweeps him off-screen ("WORTH ITwwww—").
- **BEAT 2 — MIDWAY. Mr Net Stupid Zero turns up — the level TURNS.** Caught
  mid-villainy (gold shovel / bribe / "NEW LEVY" sign). As he speaks the world
  goes **dark & grimy**. Exit: scurries off dropping coins. Dark stretch holds.
- **BEAT 3 — Ed Megawatt counters — world re-brightens.** "Make clean power the
  cheapest power and his oil's worthless." Palette warms back up. Funny exit:
  bonks off a solar panel, thumbs up.
- **BEAT 4 — PABLO opens the secret path.** PABLO (NZA's optimisation engine as a
  friendly sprite) hums in near a dead end, "optimal route found" — a hidden
  route to the boss opens.
- **BEAT 5 — The Oil Baron. The boss fight.** Atop a wheezing gas plant. **Your
  banked collectibles are ammo — fire clean-energy bolts at him**, dodge oil-gunk
  + falling levies; enough hits flips the plant green → a heat pump.
- **BEAT 6 — WIN.** Baron flees ("Three more parts to your plan!"); Ed Megawatt:
  "Pillar one: DONE." World snaps clean-blue; World 2 teased.

### 4.4 Cast
- **Goodie: Ed Megawatt** (energy minister; mission + first data + the market
  argument; comedy turbine exits).
- **Foil: Mr Net Stupid Zero** (the excuse + villainy; triggers the dark shift).
- **Helper: PABLO** (opens the secret path — stealth decodED).
- **Boss: The Oil Baron** (round 1; fought with clean-energy projectiles).

### 4.5 The atmosphere system (reusable)
A level can shift mood on a trigger: **palette** (warm/bright ↔ cold/grimy),
**lighting**, **music**. W1 uses it twice (dark on Beat 2, re-bright on Beat 3).
Built reusable so every world can change atmosphere.

## 5. How it sells decodED (never as an advert)
Feel the real problem (alone, overwhelmed, no numbers) → relief of allies + data.
Only explicit decodED moment: a soft line at the very end of World 4.
